Help & Support

Where to Find Help

Help is always available — and taking the first step to reach out is an act of real courage. Here are trusted starting points to find the support you deserve.

Types of Support

Six Pathways to Support

Medical
Talk to Your Doctor

Your primary care physician can refer you to mental health specialists, prescribe medication if appropriate, and rule out physical causes of symptoms. Always a strong first step.

Therapy
Therapy & Counseling

Licensed therapists, psychologists, and counselors offer evidence-based treatments like CBT, DBT, EMDR, and talk therapy — tailored to your specific situation and goals.

Crisis
Crisis Helplines

If you're in crisis, contact a helpline immediately. Most offer 24/7 free, confidential support by phone, text, or online chat — no appointment needed.

Digital
Mental Health Apps

Apps like Headspace, Calm, Woebot, and Wysa offer guided meditation, mood tracking, and AI-supported conversations — useful as a complement to professional care.

Community
Support Groups

Peer support groups — in person or online — connect you with others who genuinely understand your experience. They reduce isolation and build lasting community.

Information
Online Resources

Trusted sources include WHO, NAMI, Mind.org, and Mental Health America — all offering free education, self-assessment tools, and directories to local services.

Digital Tools

Recommended Mental Health Apps

These apps are not substitutes for professional care — but they're evidence-informed tools that complement your mental health journey.

Headspace

Guided meditation, sleep sounds, and mindfulness exercises. Ideal for beginners building a daily mindfulness practice from scratch.

Calm

Sleep stories, breathing exercises, and music for stress reduction. Particularly strong for sleep improvement and anxiety relief.

Woebot

An AI-powered CBT chatbot for daily check-ins, mood tracking, and cognitive behavioral tools. Available 24/7 for free emotional support.

Daylio

Micro-diary and mood tracker. Log your daily mood and activities to identify patterns and emotional triggers over time.

Wysa

An AI emotional wellbeing coach with evidence-based CBT, DBT, and mindfulness techniques. Also connects to human therapists when needed.

Bearable

A health tracker that logs symptoms, mood, sleep, medications, and activities — helping you understand what genuinely impacts your well-being.

You Deserve Support

Whether you're in crisis, struggling quietly, or simply want to be better prepared — reaching out is always the right move. Your mental health matters as much as any other aspect of your health.
